Your Invisalign Journey with Twoth

WHEN YOU ARE READY
Consultation & 3D Imaging

In person consultation and pre-treatment assessment with our experienced Invisalign clinicians which includes a 3D Scan with the Itero Scanner that can show you what your smile could look like

WITHIN 4 WEEKS
Aligners Fitted

Your Invisalign Dentist will fit small attachments onto your teeth to help the aligners stay in place and explain the aftercare procedure

EVERY 6 WEEKS
Review Appointments

Your Invisalign Dentist checks the progress of your treatment and new aligners are provided to you as you change aligners every 7 days.

IN AS LITTLE AS 12 WEEKS
Your New Smile

At this stage your teeth will have moved and your Invisalign Dentist will review your progress. If your smile isn't quite there yet, further aligners are provided to you at no extra charge.

WHEN YOU’RE DONE
Custom Retainers

You are provided with fixed wire and removable retainers to retain your teeth in their new position.

Join the Clear Revolution

No surprises

The first benefit of Invisalign over traditional braces will become clear before even beginning treatment. Our 3D imaging technology takes all of the guesswork out of the procedure, allowing us to show you a pixel-perfect picture of the finished result before you even try on your first aligner. It means you can be sure you'll love your new smile before committing.

Discreet appearance

Invisalign was the first ever orthodontic aligner to offer total discretion. Many years on, and they are still the market leader when it comes to practically invisible treatment. The secret is in the transparent material used to produce the aligners. which is barely noticeable by others. Many of our patients say even their loved- ones don't realise they are undergoing straightening treatment.

No wires

Traditional fixed braces rely on a series of wires and brackets affixed to the teeth, which can cause discomfort and draw attention where it is least wanted. The replacement of so many fixings with a single plastic retainer is an elegant and innovative solution that represents a giant leap forward in discretion, comfort and convenience.

Safer treatment

While straighter teeth are always good news for oral health, treatment with metal braces isn't always kind to the teeth. They can be especially problematic for patients engaged in contact sports or martial arts when sharp edges and wires can easily damage the mouth and gums. The metal can also act as a catalyst for tooth demineralisation and decay. Finally, the straightening forces exerted on the teeth by traditional fixed braces are much stronger than those exerted by the altogether gentler Invisalign.

Removable appliance

Generally, Invisalign aligners should only be removed for eating and cleaning. We recommend they be worn for at least 20 hours every day. This gives patients the option of removing the appliance temporarily for special events like weddings, job interviews or heart-stopping first dates.

Eat what you like

Comfort and ease of cleaning lead some wearers of traditional braces to avoid tougher foods like red meat. In contrast, Invisalign wearers pop out their straightener at mealtimes - so nothing is ever off the menu.

Perfect results

Here's the thing Invisalign patients love the most: The procedure gives perfect results every single time. If you're ready to begin your Invisalign journey, contact the team at Harley Street Dental Studio's London clinic to arrange your consultation today.
