University, USA. Fulan has lectured internationally in

London where he gained further insight into facial growth
and development and the latest rejuvenating treatments.
He is a thought leader in ‘The Art of Orthodontics’ and is
committed to healthy, beautiful smiles for adolescents
and adults.

Pain Free Dentistry with the Enjoyable Dentistry Technique

A visit to the dentist should not be painful.
So all our treatments include nitrous oxide
and oxygen sedation with relaxation instructions
and guided mediation for a pain free
relaxing experience.

It's normal to feel anxious about visiting
the dentist. We can help.

Treating nervous patients requires a high level of care to consider the apprehensions and anxiety of the patient. Too often patients suffer through tooth pain and too reluctant to visit the dentist to have it checked out of anxiety and fear of pain during treatment.
