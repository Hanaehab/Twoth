Skin Treatments to Enhance the Natural Beauty You Already Have

Dr Tara is trained in administering advanced skin treatments such as chemical peels and mesotherapy.

Mesotherapy is a minimally invasive procedure where a series of superfine injections of vitamins, minerals and amino acids cocktails are delivered into the skin. This rejuvenates the skin.

A chemical peel is where a chemical solution is applied to the skin that causes it to exfoliate and eventually peel off. Leaving you with regenerated skin that is smoother and less wrinkled. The level of chemical peel administered depends on your skin’s needs.

Aquagold Finetouch is a luxury 24 carat gold facial device that delivers a personalised blend of ingredients into the skin via 20 fine microneedles. Ingredients are chosen especially by Tara for your personal skin goals and concerns.

Skin Boosters are hydrating microinjections of a soft, gel-like substance called Hyaluronic Acid (HA) into the skin, which improves the skin’s firmness, elasticity and radiance.

Treatments

MILLION DOLLAR FACIAL

Designed to deeply exfoliate dead skin cells, remove hair, polish the skin, flush toxins, and stimulate our natural collagen and cell turnover.

DERMAPLANING

Is an exfoliating treatment that removes the top most layer of dead skin as well as the vellus hair on your face, aka, peach fuzz! Leaving you with glowing fresh skin.

LED LIGHT THERAPY

Uses bright light-emitting diodes that send low-level light energy into the deeper layers of the skin. Minimising fine lines and wrinkles, reduce redness and more!

LIPOFIRM FAT REDUCTION

Uses radio frequencies, muscle activation and heat to break down fat. The muscle activating mechanical pressures cause the muscles to contract throughout the process having firming effect..

Benefits of Mesotherapy

Promote Hair Growth

Multiple sessions are required to get to the hair growth stage, by stimulating blood flow and providing essential nutrients, the hair follicle is encouraged to produce stronger, denser hair growth.

Improve the Overall Appearance of the Skin

A typical mesotherapy blend might contain 50 ingredients to nourish, repair and rejuvenate skin. Leaving you with a complexion that is overall more refreshed, even and glowing!

Help Reduce Cellulite

By injecting fat dissolving enzymes we can use mesotherapy to help to reduce excess fat and tighten loose skin.

Address Pigmentation Issues and Scarring

By using ingredients that help to reduce unwanted pigmentation by controlling the rate of pigment production in the skin, we can target issues such as sun spots. For scarring, we can tailor the mesotherapy to include hyaluronic acid which helps add volume to the skin. Your mesotherapy is tailored to your skin needs.
